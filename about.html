An isolated small planet on the outskirts of the galaxy full of corrupted robots and technology. Fog and mist cover the whole planet with its light colour. The environment filled with high tech machines combined with the ancient ruins. The planet is going to have little lighting with dark feeling.

Lost Signal is and FPS shooter set on a mysterious, frozen planet. You play as an astronout sent to restore a long-abandoned satellite network, but the mission quickly turns into a fight for survival. As you explore ancient ruins buried beneath the snow, hostile robotic guards emerge from the mist. Will you be able to uncover the truth behind this lost civilization or will the planet's secrets remain hidden forever?

The aim is to connect different satellite wires and collect enough power to restore corrupted signals and run your spaceship again. To do so, you need to solve small puzzles and discover new areas around the planet.
