Lost Signal is an adventure game where you play as an astronaut sent to a distant planet to repair a network of ancient technology, important for communicating with Earth. As you restore the satellites, you will fight against the corrupted robots, dodge deadly lasers and fix broken wires. The fate of Earth depends on your ability to restore the corrupted signals before it is too late.

As you navigate the planet, you will find yourself surrounded by thick fog. The ancient ruins, coverent in snow and forgotten by time hold secrets of a lost civilization. Every step feels isolated, with only the sound of the wind and distant mechanical echoes breaking the silence. The deeper you go, the stronger the sense of loneliness grows, as is something unseen is watching from the shadows.
